Frequently Asked Questions

How do I connect GymReady to Hevy?

Go to Settings in GymReady and enter your Hevy API key. You can find your API key in the Hevy app under Settings > Developer. Once entered, GymReady will sync your workout history automatically.

What does GymReady do that Hevy doesn't?

Hevy is a great workout tracker. GymReady is the programming brain that sits on top of it. GymReady handles mesocycle planning, volume analytics with MEV/MAV/MRV bands, automatic progression, fatigue management, deload intelligence, and adaptive adjustments based on your feedback. It then exports your programme back to Hevy so you can train with it.

What's included in the free tier?

The free tier includes Hevy workout sync, the home dashboard, workout history, and 1 active mesocycle. Pro unlocks unlimited mesocycles, Hevy export, volume dashboard, adaptive engine, E1RM tracking, and cross-mesocycle learning.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through Apple. Go to Settings on your iPhone > tap your name > Subscriptions > GymReady > Cancel Subscription. You'll continue to have access until the end of your billing period.

Does GymReady work offline?

Yes. All your data is stored locally on your device. You only need an internet connection to sync with Hevy or export routines. Everything else works fully offline.

Can I use kg and lb?

Yes. Go to Settings to choose your preferred unit. GymReady syncs with whatever unit system you use in Hevy.

What happens to my data if I delete the app?

Deleting the app removes all local data. If you have iCloud sync enabled, your data is backed up and will restore when you reinstall. Your Hevy data remains in Hevy regardless.

How does the adaptive engine work?

After each session, you rate session RPE and per-muscle feedback (pump, soreness, joint pain). GymReady's 3-pass system then adjusts your upcoming sessions: the load pass tweaks weight if RPE was off target, the volume pass adjusts set counts based on recovery, and the RPE targets pass modifies the intensity ramp. It also monitors 10 fatigue signals to recommend deloads.

What programme templates are available?

GymReady includes 15+ templates: Push/Pull/Legs (3-6 day), Upper/Lower (4 day), Full Body (3 day), Bro Split (5 day), and more. Templates are filtered by your goal (hypertrophy, strength, powerbuilding) and available training days.
