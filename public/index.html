The brain for your strength training

GymReady doesn't replace your workout tracker. It sits on top of it. Mesocycle programming, volume analytics, fatigue management, and adaptive progression — all powered by your Hevy data.

Built for Hevy

GymReady syncs your workout history, builds your programmes, and exports them straight back to Hevy as organised routines. Two-way integration — your data flows both ways.

Auto-Sync

Your Hevy workout history syncs automatically. GymReady uses it to calculate starting weights, track compliance, and power the adaptive engine.

Export to Hevy

Your full mesocycle exports as organised Hevy routines — one folder per mesocycle, one routine per session per week. Open your next session directly in Hevy.

Planned vs. Actual

See your prescribed sets, reps, weight, and RPE next to what you actually did. Track compliance per exercise with automatic percentage scoring.

Features

Mesocycle Builder

  • 15+ programme templates — PPL, Upper/Lower, Full Body, Bro Split — filtered by goal and training days
  • Choose your goal: hypertrophy, strength, or powerbuilding
  • 5 progression types per exercise: volume, load, RPE, double progression, and Wendler 5/3/1
  • Auto-calculates starting weights from your Hevy workout history via e1RM estimation
  • Set training weeks (4-8) with optional deload week
  • Assign sessions to specific weekdays

Equipment-Aware Programming

  • Create gym profiles — home gym, commercial gym, travel setup
  • Builder auto-swaps exercises you can't do with your equipment (e.g. leg press to barbell squat, cable row to dumbbell row)
  • Manual swap option with substitution groups by movement pattern

Volume Analytics

  • Real-time volume dashboard by muscle group with MEV/MAV/MRV bands
  • Direct vs. total volume toggle — counts secondary muscle work at 0.5x
  • Colour-coded status: on target, below minimum, approaching ceiling

7-Point Science Checks

Validates your mesocycle before you commit:

  • Muscle frequency
  • Volume floor and ceiling
  • Deload planning
  • RIR ramp structure
  • Compound anchoring
  • Equipment compatibility
  • Overall programme balance

Adaptive Engine

Post-session feedback: session RPE, per-muscle pump/soreness/joint pain, performance trend.

3-pass adjustment system reacts to your feedback:

  • Load pass — adjusts weight if RPE is off target
  • Volume pass — holds or reduces sets based on recovery signals
  • RPE targets pass — flattens or steepens the intensity ramp

10-signal deload detection flags when fatigue is accumulating and recommends deload timing.

Homepage Dashboard

  • Active mesocycle progress at a glance
  • Next session card with exercise list and "Open in Hevy" button
  • Weekly volume breakdown by muscle group
  • E1RM trend sparklines for compound lifts (week-over-week)
  • Workout frequency chart
  • Recent workout history

Cross-Mesocycle Learning

  • Records your personal volume landmarks — when you needed to deload, peak volumes reached
  • Calibrates your MEV/MAV/MRV over time so each mesocycle is better than the last

Built to Work Anywhere

  • Fully offline — all data stored locally on your device
  • kg/lb unit preference
  • iCloud backup for device replacement and multi-device sync
