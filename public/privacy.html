Privacy Policy

Last updated: 4 March 2026

Overview

GymReady is designed with privacy at its core. Your workout data stays on your device. We do not operate servers that store your personal data.

Data We Collect

Data stored on your device only

  • Workout data synced from Hevy (exercise history, sets, reps, weights)
  • Mesocycle configurations you create
  • Equipment profiles
  • Session feedback and adaptive engine data
  • App preferences (units, display settings)

This data is stored locally using Apple's SwiftData framework and is never sent to our servers.

iCloud sync

If you have iCloud enabled, your GymReady data may sync across your Apple devices via Apple's CloudKit service. This is handled entirely by Apple's infrastructure and subject to Apple's Privacy Policy. We do not have access to your iCloud data.

Hevy API key

Your Hevy API key is stored securely in the iOS Keychain on your device. It is used solely to communicate with Hevy's API to sync your workout data and export routines. We never see, store, or transmit your API key.

Subscription data

Subscriptions are managed entirely by Apple via StoreKit 2. Payment information is handled by Apple and is never shared with us. We receive only the subscription status (active, expired, trial) to determine which features to unlock.

Data We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect personal information (name, email, address)
  • We do not use analytics or tracking services
  • We do not use advertising SDKs
  • We do not sell, share, or transfer any user data to third parties
  • We do not create user accounts or require registration

Third-Party Services

Hevy

GymReady connects to the Hevy API at your request to sync workout data and export routines. Data transferred is subject to Hevy's Privacy Policy. The connection uses your personal API key and is made directly from your device.

Apple

iCloud sync and subscription management are provided by Apple. See Apple's Privacy Policy.

Data Retention

All data is stored on your device. Deleting the app removes all local data. iCloud data can be managed through your Apple ID settings.

Children's Privacy

GymReady is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at support@gymready.app.
